A Note to Our Future Clients

Thank you for considering BlueScapes for your project. Before we get started, we want to be upfront about what building a quality pool in Utah looks like today.

Demand for new pool construction across Utah remains exceptionally high. Quality materials — gunite, plumbing, automation systems, and specialty finishes — continue to face supply constraints and longer lead times. Finding and retaining skilled tradespeople who meet our standards is equally competitive in today's market.

We will not rush a project to fit an unrealistic schedule. Every BlueScapes build is completed by our own in-house crew using materials we personally vet. That commitment means we take on a limited number of new projects at a time — and we give each one the attention it deserves.

1–2 Weeks

Step 2
4–8 Weeks
3D design, permitting & approvals
Step 3

Step 4

New pool construction in Utah typically starts at $75K–$100K and scales with size, style, and features. Most clients plan 9–12 months from first contact to completion when accounting for design, permitting, and build time. We will give you a realistic start date during your consultation — not a number designed just to win your business. Submitting this form reserves your place in our scheduling queue.

We genuinely appreciate your trust in BlueScapes. We look forward to building something extraordinary for your family.
